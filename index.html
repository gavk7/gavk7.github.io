Gavin Keir

Currently a Learning Design Officer at the University of Queensland.

Hi, my name is Gavin Keir. I am currently living in South-East Queensland and have been working as a Learning Designer across a number of faculties and schools at the University of Queensland since mid-late 2020. In my current role, I provide key support to the UQx Learning Design team for the delivery of online courses on the edX and UQx Edge online learning platforms.

I have extensive past experience in curriculum development, pedagogy and assessment, and have taught both overseas and in Australia in secondary and tertiary educational institutions./

I have a keen interest in teaching pedagogy, insights gained from learners and their learning experience, and ways to enhance teaching and learning through the use of technology.

The best way to reach me is to either connect with me on LinkedIn or email me at gavink7@yahoo.com
