Gavin Keir

Currently a Learning Designer building online courses at the University of Queensland.

    

Hi, my name is Gavin Keir.  I have extensive past experience in curriculum development, pedagogy and assessment, and have taught both overseas and in Australia in secondary and tertiary educational institutions.  In my current role , I provide key support to the UQx Learning Design team for the delivery of online courses on the edX and UQx Edge online learning platforms.

In my previous position, I coordinated and managed the daily operations of the English Language Centre at the CQUniversity Brisbane campus for a number of years as the Director of Studies there.

➤ I was engaged in: 1) classroom teaching and teaching support; 2) academic counselling to students, student monitoring, identification of at-risk students, student grading and reporting, and marking and moderation of assessments; 3) providing guidance and support for our teachers, hiring teachers, providing teacher training, teacher observations and feedback, and hosting professional development workshops; 4) improving course materials to better support both teachers and students in the classroom to provide for enhanced learning experiences, curriculum materials and assessment improvements; and 5) utilising innovative technologies in the classroom for both teaching and learning environments.

My current professional interests are in teaching pedagogy, insights gained from learners and their learning experience, and finding new ways to enhance teaching and learning through the use of technology.
