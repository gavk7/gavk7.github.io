Gavin Keir

Currently a Learning Design Officer at the University of Queensland.

Hi - My name is Gavin! Currently, I am living in South East Queensland and have been working as a Learning Designer on short term contracts across a range of Faculty Schools at the University of Queensland since mid-late 2020. Currently, I am working for the UQx team providing key support to our learning designer team producing online courses on EDx and UQx Edge platforms. Previously, I worked for many years at CQUniversity, Brisbane Campus as the Director of Studies, and managed all aspects of running the English language centre there.

I have an extensive background in as an English teacher, and have taught both overseas and in Australia in secondary and tertiary educational institutions. I have a keen interest in teaching pedagogy, insights gained from learners and their learning experience, and ways to enhance teaching and learning through the use of technology.

The best way to reach me is to either connect with me on LinkedIn or email me at gavink7@yahoo.com
